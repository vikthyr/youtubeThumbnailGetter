Where is thumbnail saved after being downloaded?

It all depends on the Browser and operating system you are using, but normally all thumbnail images are saved under DOWNLOADS folder on Mac and Windows. You can also find by press CTRL+J in your browser to check your download history.


If you are using a mobile browser, you can view your download history just tap on OPTIONS and then on DOWNLOADS.

What is YouTube thumbnail

Youtube thumbnail is a type of image that represents a video before you click it, YouTube thumbnail is a summary of this video that is in the form of an image, before clicking on it, we have an idea of the content to be presented.
