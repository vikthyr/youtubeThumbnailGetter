What is YouTube Thumbnail Getter

YouTube Thumbnail Getter is an online web tool where you can download any YouTube video's thumbnail easily. With the help of this tool you can easily download YouTube thumbnail in different sizes and qualities.


How to use YouTube Thumbnail Getter

  1. Enter YouTube URL or ID in the field.
  2. Click on the GET THUMBNAIL button.
  3. Click the button with the RESOLUTION you want to download.
  4. Save the file to your DEVICE.

Where is thumbnail saved after being downloaded?

It all depends on the Browser and operating system you are using, but normally all thumbnail images are saved under DOWNLOADS folder on Mac and Windows. You can also find by press CTRL+J in your browser to check your download history.


If you are using a mobile browser, you can view your download history just tap on OPTIONS and then on DOWNLOADS.
